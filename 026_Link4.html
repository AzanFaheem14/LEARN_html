There are different types of computer:

  • Supercomputer
  • Minicomputer
  • Personal computer
  • Workstation
  • Laptop

Supercomputer:

A high-performance computer that's measured by floating-point operations per second (FLOPS).







Minicomputer:

A mid-sized computer with multiple processors that can support hundreds of users simultaneously.







Personal Computer

A digital device for personal use, such as studying, working, gaming, and browsing the internet.







Workstation:

A computer that's configured to meet demanding technical computing requirements.







Laptop:

A portable computer that is designed to be similar desktop computer.
